Dangers of Vaping

  • Exposure to harmful chemicals like formaldehyde and acetaldehyde
  • Highly addictive due to nicotine content
  • Linked to long-term respiratory and cardiovascular disease
  • Potential gateway to cigarette smoking, especially in youth
  • Unregulated products may contain toxic or unknown substances

Key UK Stats

  • 1 in 7 teens report daily vaping by age 15
  • Over 25% of vapers never smoked before
  • Almost all vapes sold in the UK contain nicotine

Information for Schools

We partner with schools to deliver up-to-date education on vaping harms. We offer curriculum-aligned lesson plans, printable posters, peer-led intervention models and teacher CPD resources. Contact your local authority or email info@vapeaware.org.uk for more information.
